We are an experimental atomic physics group at [Old Dominion University](https://www.odu.edu/physics) working at the intersection of **quantum information** and **precision measurement**. Our experiments use trapped ions as a platform for studying fundamental physics and building quantum technologies.

Our research interests span ion trap design and fabrication, quantum state control and readout, and high-precision spectroscopy. We are part of the broader atomic, molecular, and optical (AMO) physics community and collaborate with groups across the country.

**Prospective students:** We are always looking for motivated graduate and undergraduate students. See the [Research](/research/) page for a summary of what we are working on, and feel free to [get in touch](mailto:mgrau@odu.edu).
