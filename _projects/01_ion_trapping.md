We design and build custom ion traps tailored to our experimental needs. Our traps confine individual atomic ions using a combination of static and oscillating electric fields, allowing us to isolate and manipulate single quantum systems.

**Key activities:**
- Trap design and simulation (finite-element modeling of electrode geometries)
- Fabrication of surface and 3D traps
- Laser cooling and loading of ion species
- Motional mode characterization and control
