Trapped ions are among the most advanced platforms for quantum information processing. We work on implementing high-fidelity quantum gates, preparing entangled states, and developing protocols for quantum computation and simulation.

**Key activities:**
- Single and two-qubit gate implementations
- State preparation and readout
- Entanglement generation and characterization
- Quantum error mitigation
