The exceptional isolation and controllability of trapped ions makes them ideal for precision measurements. We use atomic transitions as ultra-stable frequency references and as sensitive probes for new physics beyond the standard model.

**Key activities:**
- High-resolution spectroscopy of narrow atomic transitions
- Systematic effect characterization and mitigation
- Tests of fundamental symmetries
- Searches for physics beyond the standard model
